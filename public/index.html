信東電子有限公司（DIAMONDEL Electronics Co., Ltd.）成立於台灣新北市，長期深耕全球電子零件獨立經銷市場，客戶遍及製造業、系統整合商及電子產品設計公司。 DIAMONDEL Electronics Co., Ltd., based in New Taipei City, Taiwan, has been a trusted independent IC distributor for over two decades, serving manufacturers, system integrators, and design houses worldwide.

憑藉完善的全球供應商網絡，即使在市場缺料情況下，我們也能迅速找到客戶所需元件，提供具競爭力的報價與穩定交期保障。 With an extensive global supplier network, we can source components quickly even during shortages, delivering competitive pricing and reliable lead times.
